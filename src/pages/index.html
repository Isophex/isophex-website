Helios IPM

Helios Intellectual Property is a law service technologies company that specializes in IP management. They came to us in need of a complete redesign of both their website and overall branding. As a new startup, Helios needed to stand out and convey new ideas to a more conventual audience. Taking into consideration the fact that it was a business to business (B2B) site, our solution included a more traditional highly functional design that conveyed a “new brighter way to manage IP operations."
